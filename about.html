PLANT

Plants have beauty, shade, and a clean environment that give the place where they are located a special joy and elegance, but what are the ornamental plants suitable for cultivation? What are the types of house plants? What is suitable for the garden and what is suitable for the home? Which plants produce roses? Then how do I organize my home garden?…etc. One of the many questions that the plant-lover is always looking for, and which we are trying to answer through this website. My Plants website started in 2014 as an educational website that provides information about indoor and outdoor ornamental plants due to the lack of Arabic sources and the large number of misinformation, which reinforces the idea that acquiring plants is a difficult task that requires specialists in their breeding.
